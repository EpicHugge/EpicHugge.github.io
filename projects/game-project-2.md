      <div class="card-text">
        <h2><strong>Design</strong></h2>
        <p>
          The <strong>hook ability</strong> was originally intended for reaching high platforms, 
          but this conflicted with enemy AI, as they couldn't follow the player. 
          To fix this, I repositioned <strong>hook anchors</strong> to focus on <strong>speed 
          and traversal</strong> rather than vertical movement, allowing for 
          <strong>fluid mobility</strong> without breaking combat flow.
        </p>
        <p>
          Playtesting led to the <strong>removal of excess platforms</strong> to keep 
          the brawler focus intact. I also fine-tuned <strong>ambush sections 
          and enemy placement</strong> to maintain a balanced difficulty curve 
          despite frequent AI tweaks.
        </p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="portfolio-sidecard">

          <strong>combat, traversal, and level flow</strong>. Frequent <strong>enemy AI tweaks</strong> 
          required ongoing adjustments to <strong>enemy placement and ambush pacing</strong> 
          to maintain fair difficulty.
        </p>

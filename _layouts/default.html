Level Design Assignment

Level Summary

Set in feudal Japan, this level takes place in a tense, heavily guarded gatehouse—the village’s main checkpoint and defense post. The player must sneak through the multi-level structure, avoiding patrols while searching for a way to unlock the main gate and escape.

Designed with verticality and multiple paths, the gatehouse offers both stealth and combat opportunities, depending on the player’s choices. Environmental storytelling is woven throughout, revealing glimpses of confiscated goods, a holding cell, and a smuggler’s fate. Every detail is placed with intent, creating a tight, immersive space that rewards exploration and strategic movement.

Narrative

Framed for his captain’s murder, Date Nasanube (or Senji Tome) must escape the village and turn the people against General Oda Nobunaga by burning the grain storage. His path leads through the heavily guarded gatehouse, where stealth and strategy are key.

The environment tells its own story—a prisoner, Ushi Maki Ramen, has been caught smuggling goods and offers a secret escape in exchange for help. Meanwhile, guards openly misuse confiscated wares, exposing the corruption within. Players can choose their approach: sneak through hidden paths or fight their way to freedom.
